Fat Dissolving Injections

If you’ve been struggling with stubborn areas of fat that just won’t budge — no matter how much you diet or exercise — our fat dissolving injections offer a non-surgical solution. At PDC Aesthetics, we use industry-leading products to safely target areas like the chin, arms, stomach, or thighs.

The procedure is quick, minimally invasive, and effective over a series of treatments. It works by breaking down fat cells, which are naturally removed by the body over time. There’s minimal downtime, and visible results can start appearing within weeks.

We conduct a full consultation to assess your suitability and desired outcome. Our treatments are discreet, precise, and always performed with care in a relaxing, private setting.

Microneedling for Skin Renewal

Microneedling is a powerful skin rejuvenation treatment that stimulates natural collagen production. At PDC Aesthetics, we use precision micro-needling devices to create controlled micro-injuries on the skin’s surface, encouraging your body’s healing response.

This technique can dramatically improve skin texture, reduce the appearance of acne scars, fine lines, and enlarged pores. It also helps skincare products absorb deeper, making your post-treatment skincare routine more effective.

We personalise every microneedling session to suit your skin type and concern. Whether you’re looking to refresh dull skin or target deeper texture issues, our microneedling service is an ideal, non-invasive option with lasting results.

Dermaplaning for Smooth, Glowing Skin

Dermaplaning is a gentle exfoliation treatment that removes dead skin cells and fine vellus hair (peach fuzz) using a sterile, medical-grade blade. The result? Instantly smoother, brighter skin and a flawless base for makeup.

This luxurious treatment is perfect before events or as part of a regular skincare routine. It’s suitable for most skin types and provides immediate visible results with no downtime.

At PDC Aesthetics, we pair dermaplaning with nourishing aftercare to leave your skin glowing and hydrated. It’s one of our most popular entry-level treatments — simple, effective, and indulgent.

Our tailored skin peels are designed to exfoliate the upper layers of the skin, improving texture, tone, and clarity. Whether you suffer from pigmentation, acne, or dullness, a skin peel can help reveal fresher, healthier skin beneath.

We offer a range of chemical peels to suit different skin types and concerns. From gentle exfoliating formulas to deeper, active treatments, we’ll recommend the right level of intensity for your goals and skin health.

Every peel at PDC Aesthetics is followed by expert soothing and hydration to ensure optimal recovery and results. Let your glow shine through with this powerful, science-backed skin solution.

Skin Booster for Deep Hydration

Skin boosters are injectable treatments that deliver intense hydration and bio-stimulation deep within the skin. They’re ideal for tackling dryness, dullness, fine lines, and skin laxity — especially around the face, neck, and décolletage.

Unlike fillers, skin boosters don’t alter facial volume. Instead, they improve skin quality from the inside out, making your complexion smoother, plumper, and more youthful over time.

Our advanced formulations like Profhilo and Seventy Hyal are administered by trained aesthetic professionals, ensuring both safety and visible results. A true game-changer for long-term skin health.
