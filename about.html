Fat Dissolving Injections

Target tiny, stubborn pockets of fat (e.g., chin or lower belly) with minimally invasive injections designed to help refine localised areas alongside healthy lifestyle choices.

Convenient injections at home to support energy and overall wellbeing as part of a balanced lifestyle. We’ll advise on frequency during your consultation.

Mobile Aesthetics: Frequently Asked Questions

Is mobile aesthetics as safe as a clinic?

Yes — when delivered by a trained practitioner using sterile equipment and proper protocols. We follow strict hygiene, screening and aftercare standards on every visit. Learn more on About Us.

Which areas do you cover?

We’re based in Stone and travel roughly 20 miles to nearby towns and villages (e.g., Stafford, Eccleshall, Barlaston, Trentham, Newcastle-under-Lyme, Cheadle). If you’re just outside the radius, check our coverage or send a message to see if we can accommodate.

How long does a typical appointment take?

Most appointments last 45–60 minutes including consultation and aftercare. Some treatments (like microneedling or peels) may take a little longer — we’ll advise when booking.

What do I need to prepare at home?

A clean, well-lit space such as a table or worktop is ideal. We bring everything else and keep set-up tidy and efficient.

Do you offer anti-wrinkle (Botox) at home?

Yes — see our dedicated Anti-Wrinkle (Botox) page for what’s involved, typical areas, and aftercare.

Can I combine treatments?

Absolutely. Many clients combine dermaplaning with peels, or pair microneedling with skin boosters across a plan. We’ll tailor suggestions during your consultation.

What are your prices?

See transparent starting prices on our Pricing page. For bundled plans or group bookings in one location, ask for a personalised quote.

Is there a travel fee?

Travel within ~20 miles of Stone is typically included. If you’re beyond this, we’ll advise of any small surcharge during booking.

Do you offer group or pair bookings?

Yes — home visits are ideal for two or more people in the same location. It can be convenient and cost-effective; just mention it when you enquire.
