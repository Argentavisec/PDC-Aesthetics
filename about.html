Frequently Asked Questions – Mobile Aesthetic Clinic

Is mobile Botox safe?

Yes. When administered by a qualified professional, mobile Botox treatments are just as safe as those in clinics. Our nurse uses medical-grade supplies and follows strict hygiene protocols.

Do you offer mobile dermal fillers?

Absolutely. We provide mobile lip fillers, nasolabial fillers, and skin boosters — all from the comfort of your home.

Where do you offer mobile aesthetics?

We serve clients within 15 miles of Stone, Stoke-on-Trent including Stafford, Cheadle, Barlaston, and surrounding towns.

Why choose a mobile aesthetic nurse over a clinic?

Mobile services offer more privacy, flexibility, and comfort. They're ideal for parents, busy professionals, and anyone who prefers a quiet, one-on-one setting.

Is there an extra charge for mobile visits?

No extra cost within our standard coverage zone. If you’re slightly outside it, we may still be able to accommodate you — just get in touch.
