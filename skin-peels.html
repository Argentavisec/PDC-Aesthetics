What is a Skin Peel?

A skin peel uses a professional-grade chemical solution to gently exfoliate the top layers of the skin. This encourages fresh, new skin cells to surface, leaving behind smoother, clearer, and brighter skin. Peels can vary in strength depending on your concern — from superficial glow-boosters to deeper corrective formulas.

What to Expect During Treatment

After a thorough consultation and skin cleanse, the selected peel is applied for a set time and neutralised. You may feel mild tingling or warmth, but discomfort is minimal. The treatment lasts around 30 minutes in total and can easily be done at home without needing to travel to a clinic.

Aftercare & Results

You may experience slight flaking or redness after the treatment, but this is usually mild and short-lived. We provide full aftercare instructions and follow up to monitor results. Most clients notice improvements after a single session, with ongoing results building over time — especially when booked as a course.

Skin Peel FAQs – Everything You Need to Know

Are skin peels safe?

Yes — when performed by a qualified practitioner. At PDC Aesthetics, we use only medically approved peel solutions and tailor the strength to your skin’s condition and tolerance.

How long does it take to see results?

You may notice a brighter complexion within a few days. For longer-term concerns like pigmentation or acne, a series of peels may be recommended for best results.

Is there any downtime?

Mild redness or dryness may occur, but most clients return to normal activities straight after the treatment. Makeup can be worn 24–48 hours post-treatment if needed.

Can I combine a peel with other treatments?

Yes — many clients combine peels with treatments like microneedling or dermaplaning. We’ll guide you on the best treatment plan based on your goals.
