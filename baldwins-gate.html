Frequently Asked Questions – Baldwins Gate Clients

Do I need to travel for treatments?

No. We offer fully mobile services — treatments are carried out in the comfort of your own home in Baldwins Gate.

Is mobile Botox as safe as clinic-based?

Yes. All our mobile Botox treatments are performed by a registered nurse with full qualifications and insurance.

Absolutely. We provide daytime, evening, and weekend appointments to suit your schedule.

How long do aesthetic treatments last?

It depends on the treatment. Botox typically lasts 3–4 months, fillers around 6–12 months, and skin boosters up to 6 months.

Yes. Every appointment includes a consultation where we discuss your goals, answer questions, and ensure the treatment is right for you.
