Aqualyx vs Deso — What’s the Difference?

Aqualyx®

Established option for small, stubborn pockets

  • Used for submental (double chin) and body areas
  • Often planned in 2–4 sessions, 4–6 weeks apart
  • Swelling/tenderness expected for a few days post-treatment

Deso® (DesoFace / DesoBody)

Formulations tailored for face vs body

  • DesoFace for delicate submental/jowl areas; DesoBody for larger zones
  • Typically a course of sessions based on area size
  • Comfort measures and aftercare provided to support recovery

We’ll recommend the product and course that suit your anatomy, timeline and expectations.

Who It’s For (and Not For)

  • Best for: small, localised, pinchable fat that resists diet/training
  • Common sites: double chin, abdomen, flanks, upper arms, bra/back fat
  • Not ideal for: weight loss, significant skin laxity, or wide-area contouring
  • We’ll assess your goals, medical history and skin to confirm suitability

Course Planner & Timeline

  1. Consultation & Mark-Up — anatomy review, photos and plan.
  2. Session 1 — quick treatment; swelling/heat/tenderness are expected.
  3. Session 2–4 (4–6 weeks apart) — builds cumulative improvement.
  4. Review — we assess response and adjust if needed.
  5. Maintenance — not always required; lifestyle supports results.

Complementary options: Combine with skin boosters (skin quality) or microneedling (texture) for a holistic glow-up.

Aftercare (Chin & Body)

  • First 24–48 hours: compression as advised, avoid heat and strenuous exercise
  • Expect swelling and tenderness; intermittent cool compress can soothe
  • Keep the area clean; avoid alcohol for 24 hours
  • Sleep with head slightly elevated for chin treatments
  • We’ll check in and plan next-session timing (usually 4–6 weeks)
