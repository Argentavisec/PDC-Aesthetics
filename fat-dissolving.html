What Are Fat Dissolving Injections?

Fat dissolving injections use a naturally occurring compound called deoxycholic acid which breaks down and flushes out fat cells in targeted areas. Once treated, the fat cells are permanently removed and cannot return.

This treatment is ideal for clients who are close to their ideal weight but struggle with small, stubborn pockets of fat that resist diet and exercise.

  • Non-surgical, needle-based treatment
  • No general anaesthetic required
  • Minimal downtime
  • Visible results after just a few sessions

What to Expect During Your Appointment

During your consultation, we’ll assess your goals and discuss realistic expectations. The injection itself takes around 20–30 minutes and is relatively painless — some minor swelling, redness, or tenderness may occur but typically fades within a few days.

Fat dissolving results are gradual, as the body naturally flushes the treated fat over several weeks. Most clients see optimal results after 2–4 sessions spaced several weeks apart.

Frequently Asked Questions – Fat Dissolving Injections

Are fat dissolving injections safe?

Yes. When administered by a qualified nurse, fat dissolving injections like Aqualyx are considered safe and widely used in aesthetics. We use CE-approved products and follow strict medical protocols.

How many sessions will I need?

Most clients need between 2 and 5 sessions depending on the size of the area and your body’s response. We’ll create a tailored plan during your consultation.

What areas can be treated?

Common areas include chin, jowls, stomach, flanks (love handles), thighs, upper arms, bra bulge, and back fat. If you’re unsure, we’ll assess suitability during your assessment.

Are fat dissolving injections painful?

You may feel some stinging or burning during injection, but we use numbing to reduce discomfort. Most clients report mild soreness or tightness afterward which subsides within days.

What should I do after treatment?

Avoid vigorous exercise, heat, and alcohol for 24–48 hours. Follow all aftercare guidance we provide. Swelling, redness, and minor bruising are normal in the days following treatment.

Is there any downtime?

Downtime is minimal. You can usually return to normal activity straight away, but we suggest avoiding social events for 1–2 days if swelling is visible.

Can fat return after treatment?

Fat cells treated with Aqualyx are permanently destroyed, but maintaining a healthy lifestyle is important to preserve results. We’ll offer advice to support you.
