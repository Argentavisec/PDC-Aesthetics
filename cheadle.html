FAQs – Aesthetic Treatments in Cheadle

Is it safe to have aesthetic treatments done at home?

Yes. All treatments are carried out by a qualified nurse with full insurance and clinical experience. We follow strict hygiene and safety protocols for every visit.

Do you offer consultations before treatment?

Absolutely. Every treatment begins with a personalised consultation to discuss your goals and medical history.

How long do Botox and fillers last?

Botox typically lasts 3–4 months. Fillers can last 6–12 months depending on the area treated and your individual metabolism.

Is there any downtime?

Most treatments involve minimal downtime. Some clients may experience redness, swelling, or bruising, which usually settles quickly.
