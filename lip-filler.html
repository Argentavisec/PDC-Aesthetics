Lip Filler Aftercare Tips

Aftercare is simple but important for ensuring the best results. We recommend:

  • Avoid touching or massaging the lips for 24 hours
  • Apply a cool compress to reduce any swelling or bruising
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, hot baths, or alcohol for 24 hours
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated
  • Sleep slightly elevated on the first night

You’ll receive a full aftercare guide after your treatment. We also follow up with every client to ensure you’re happy and healing well.
