Why Choose Lip Fillers?

Lip fillers are a fast, safe, and non-surgical way to enhance the shape, volume, and definition of your lips. Whether you're looking to add volume, restore symmetry, or smooth fine lines, dermal fillers can help you achieve a soft, balanced look tailored to your face.

  • Enhance natural lip volume
  • Correct lip asymmetry
  • Smooth vertical lines ("smoker’s lines")
  • Improve lip shape and definition
  • Restore lost fullness due to ageing

We focus on natural-looking results — enhancing your features, not changing them. You remain completely in control of your desired outcome.

You’ll notice an immediate difference after your lip filler injection. However, your final result will appear within 3–5 days once any minor swelling or bruising settles.

Most clients enjoy natural, fuller-looking lips that enhance facial balance without looking “overdone.” Results typically last 6–12 months depending on your metabolism, lifestyle, and filler type.

Lip Filler Aftercare

To ensure the best results and minimise side effects, follow these lip filler aftercare tips:

  • Avoid touching or massaging the lips for 24 hours
  • Use a cool pack to reduce swelling if needed
  • Avoid intense exercise and alcohol for 24 hours
  • Stay hydrated to help the filler settle
  • Sleep with your head slightly elevated on the first night

We'll provide full aftercare guidance during your appointment and follow up with you afterward to check in.

Frequently Asked Questions About Lip Fillers

Do lip fillers hurt?

Most clients say lip fillers are only mildly uncomfortable. We use a numbing cream before the treatment to minimise pain. The filler itself also contains a numbing agent to keep discomfort to a minimum.

Are lip fillers safe?

Yes — when administered by a qualified medical professional, lip fillers are very safe. At PDC Aesthetics, our nurse has over 29 years of clinical experience and uses CE-certified products that meet UK safety standards.

How long do lip fillers last?

On average, lip fillers last between 6 to 12 months depending on your metabolism, lifestyle, and the type of filler used. Many clients return for top-ups every 6–9 months to maintain results.

Will my lips look fake or unnatural?

Absolutely not. Our approach is always to enhance your natural beauty — not to change your appearance. You can choose a very subtle volume increase or a slightly plumper look depending on your personal preference.

What are the side effects of lip filler?

The most common side effects are temporary swelling, redness, and occasional bruising. These usually subside within a couple of days. Rare risks such as allergic reaction or lumps are discussed during your consultation.

Can I dissolve my lip filler if I don’t like it?

Yes, hyaluronic acid-based fillers are reversible using an enzyme called hyaluronidase. We can discuss this in your consultation if you’ve had filler elsewhere and are unhappy with your results.
