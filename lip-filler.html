How Lip Fillers Work

Step 1: Consultation

We begin with a full consultation to discuss your goals. Whether you want hydration, volume, or subtle shaping, we’ll design a plan that suits your face and lifestyle.

Step 2: Treatment

Using a fine needle or cannula, dermal filler is carefully injected into the lips. We can target specific areas such as the cupid’s bow, vermilion border, or overall volume.

Step 3: Aftercare

We provide clear aftercare advice to ensure your results last and healing is smooth. Swelling is minimal and usually subsides within a few days.
