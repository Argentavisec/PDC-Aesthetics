What is Dermaplaning?

Dermaplaning is a gentle, non-invasive skincare treatment that exfoliates the skin and removes dead skin cells and peach fuzz using a sterile surgical scalpel. The result? Instantly smoother, brighter skin that allows makeup to glide on flawlessly and skincare to absorb better.

This treatment is ideal for anyone looking to refresh dull skin, reduce fine lines, and achieve a natural glow without downtime or discomfort.

Who is Dermaplaning For?

Dermaplaning is suitable for almost all skin types, especially those dealing with dry, rough, or dull skin. It’s an excellent choice for people who want:

  • Brighter, healthier-looking skin
  • Gentle exfoliation without chemicals
  • Improved makeup application
  • To remove unwanted peach fuzz

It’s not recommended for those with active acne or certain skin conditions — we’ll assess suitability during your consultation.

FAQs – Dermaplaning at Home

Does dermaplaning hurt?

No. Dermaplaning is a completely painless procedure that feels like a gentle scraping. Most clients find it relaxing and enjoyable.

Will it make hair grow back thicker?

No — this is a common myth. Dermaplaning does not affect the structure or thickness of your hair. It will grow back at the same rate and texture.

How often should I get dermaplaning?

We recommend every 4 to 6 weeks depending on your skin’s needs and goals. Regular treatments help maintain a smooth, glowing complexion.

Is there any downtime?

No. You can go about your day immediately after dermaplaning. Just avoid heavy makeup or sun exposure for 24 hours.

Is dermaplaning suitable before events?

Yes! It's perfect before weddings, parties, or photo sessions — your skin will look smoother and makeup will apply flawlessly.
