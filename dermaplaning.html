Reveal Fresh, Glowing Skin with Dermaplaning

Dermaplaning is a safe, non-invasive treatment that uses a sterile blade to gently exfoliate the top layer of dead skin and remove fine vellus hair (peach fuzz). The result? Brighter, smoother, and softer skin that absorbs skincare products more effectively and creates a flawless base for makeup.

At PDC Aesthetics, dermaplaning is performed by a qualified nurse with over 29 years of clinical experience, ensuring maximum safety, hygiene, and results.

Benefits of Dermaplaning

  • Instantly smoother and more radiant skin
  • Improves product absorption and effectiveness
  • Reduces appearance of fine lines and dullness
  • Safe for most skin types
  • No downtime or discomfort

It’s the perfect pre-event glow-up or monthly maintenance treatment to keep your skin looking refreshed and healthy.

Is Dermaplaning Right for You?

Dermaplaning is suitable for most skin types and is ideal for clients who want to improve skin texture, tone, and overall radiance without the use of harsh chemicals. It is not recommended for those with active acne or extremely sensitive skin — but we’ll assess your skin during your consultation to recommend the best approach for you.
