What Happens During Dermaplaning?

After cleansing your skin thoroughly, we use a sterile surgical blade at a 45-degree angle to gently scrape away layers of dull, dead skin. The treatment is painless, relaxing, and usually takes around 30 minutes. Most clients describe it as a gentle tickle.

You’ll be able to resume your normal routine immediately afterward — no redness, no irritation, no peeling. Just smooth, clean skin that glows.

Who Is Dermaplaning For?

Dermaplaning is ideal for anyone looking to improve skin texture, radiance, and smoothness. It’s especially beneficial if you:

  • Have dry or rough skin
  • Struggle with dull complexion
  • Want to reduce fine lines and acne scars
  • Wear makeup and want it to apply more smoothly

It is safe for most skin types and can be combined with other treatments such as a chemical skin peel or skin booster for even greater results.

How Often Should I Get Dermaplaning?

We typically recommend dermaplaning every 4 to 6 weeks depending on your skin type and goals. This allows time for your skin to naturally regenerate and ensures consistent, long-term results.

Regular treatments can help maintain a vibrant glow and keep your skincare products working more effectively by eliminating the buildup that can block absorption.

Dermaplaning FAQs

Will hair grow back thicker?
No – dermaplaning removes vellus hair (peach fuzz), which will grow back at the same rate and texture.

Is it painful?
Not at all. Most clients find it soothing and relaxing. It’s often compared to a light scraping or brushing sensation.

How soon will I see results?
Immediately! Your skin will feel soft and appear brighter right after your appointment.
