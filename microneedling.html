How It Works

Microneedling involves using a sterile device with ultrafine needles to create microchannels in the skin. This triggers your body’s natural healing response, encouraging new collagen production and faster skin turnover. We apply numbing cream beforehand to ensure maximum comfort.

After the procedure, you may experience slight redness (like mild sunburn), which typically fades within 24–48 hours. Most clients report a noticeable glow and skin softness even after the first session.

Aftercare & Results

We’ll provide full aftercare guidance to help your skin heal smoothly and maximise your results. Avoid direct sun exposure, heavy makeup, and strong skincare products for the first 48 hours. Hydration and gentle skincare are key during recovery.

Microneedling FAQ – What Clients Ask Most

Is microneedling painful?

Most clients describe it as a mild prickling sensation. We use numbing cream to make the procedure as comfortable as possible.

How long do microneedling results last?

Results can last several months, especially with a proper skincare routine. Maintenance treatments are recommended every 3–6 months.

Can I wear makeup after microneedling?

It’s best to avoid makeup for at least 24–48 hours post-treatment to let your skin heal and breathe.

Is microneedling safe for all skin types?

Yes. Microneedling is generally safe for all skin tones and types, but a consultation helps us confirm the best approach for you.

How soon will I see results?

You may notice a glow within a few days, with continued improvement over the next few weeks as collagen builds.
