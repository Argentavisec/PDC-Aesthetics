How Microneedling Works

We create precise micro-channels that trigger your body’s natural healing cascade. Collagen and elastin production increases, gradually improving smoothness, firmness and clarity. Numbing is used for comfort; most people feel a mild prickling.

  • Visible glow: often within days; texture refines over weeks
  • Downtime: pinkness for 24–48 hours; avoid heat, alcohol & strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Aftercare: gentle hydration + SPF; pause actives briefly (retinoids/acids)
