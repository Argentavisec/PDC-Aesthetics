What Are Vitamin B12 Injections?

Vitamin B12, also known as cobalamin, is a vital nutrient that helps keep your body’s nerve and blood cells healthy. It also plays a key role in preventing anaemia and supports DNA synthesis, mood balance, and metabolic function. Many adults are deficient — often without knowing it.

Unlike oral supplements, B12 injections deliver the vitamin directly into your bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system. This allows for faster, more effective absorption — making it the preferred option for people needing immediate or regular energy support.

Frequently Asked Questions – Vitamin B12 Injections

Is vitamin B12 really effective for boosting energy?

Yes. Vitamin B12 plays a crucial role in converting food into energy. When you're deficient, fatigue and brain fog are common. A direct injection bypasses digestion and ensures rapid absorption, which can restore energy levels and mental clarity fast.

Are your B12 shots suitable for vegans?

Absolutely. Many vegans and vegetarians are at risk of B12 deficiency due to the lack of animal products in their diet. Our B12 injections are suitable and effective for plant-based clients.

Can B12 injections help with low mood or anxiety?

There’s growing evidence linking B12 deficiency to symptoms of depression, anxiety, and low mood. While not a substitute for mental health treatment, B12 can support emotional wellbeing when levels are low.
