Vitamin B12 Injections at PDC Aesthetics

Feeling tired, sluggish or run down? A Vitamin B12 injection could be exactly what you need. B12 is essential for energy production, brain function, and maintaining a healthy nervous system. When your B12 levels are low, symptoms can include fatigue, poor concentration, irritability, and even low mood.

At PDC Aesthetics, all B12 injections are administered by a fully qualified nurse with over 29 years of clinical experience. We make the process simple, professional, and highly effective — with many clients reporting significant improvements after just one treatment.

How It Works

The process is quick and virtually pain-free. After a brief consultation, the injection is given into the upper arm or thigh. It takes just a few minutes and you'll be on your way — with benefits that can last for several weeks.

Some clients choose a single injection when they feel rundown, while others schedule regular monthly boosts as part of their wellness routine.

Who Can Benefit from B12 Injections?

While anyone can benefit from a B12 boost, those who often report the most improvement include:

  • Busy professionals
  • New parents
  • People with low energy or brain fog
  • Vegetarians and vegans
  • Older adults with decreased absorption
  • People recovering from illness or stress
