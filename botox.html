What Can Botox Treat?

Core Expression Lines

Forehead Lines

Smooth horizontal lines while preserving natural lift.

Frown (Glabella)

Soften “11s” to ease a tired or stern look.

Crow’s Feet

Feather-light dosing to soften smile lines at the eye corners.

Refinement Areas

Bunny Lines

Reduce nose scrunch lines.

Lip Flip

Subtle upper-lip eversion if you prefer not to use filler.

DAO (Downturned Corners)

Soften downward pull at mouth corners.

Advanced Options

Masseter Slimming

Refines the lower face over time in suitable candidates.

Gummy Smile

Conservative micro-dosing to reduce gum show.

Neck Bands

Targets platysmal bands (eligibility assessed case-by-case).

How Botox Works & What to Expect

  • 3–7 days: early softening
  • ~2 weeks: best result (review included)
  • 3–4 months: typical duration (varies by area, dose & metabolism)

Aftercare Essentials

  • Stay upright for 4 hours; avoid rubbing that day
  • Avoid heavy exercise, heat and alcohol for 24 hours
  • We provide written aftercare and a check-in
