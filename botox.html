How Botox Works & What to Expect

Treatment Journey

1) Consultation & Assessment

We discuss your goals, medical history and muscle activity. You’ll get straightforward advice and a realistic, conservative plan.

2) Treatment (10–20 Minutes)

Precise micro-injections with ultra-fine needles. Most describe a mild pinch. You can return to normal daily activities immediately.

3) Timeline & Longevity

  • 3–7 days: early softening
  • ~2 weeks: best result
  • 3–4 months: typical duration (varies by metabolism, dose and area)

Comfort & Aftercare Essentials

  • Avoid heavy exercise, heat and alcohol for 24 hours
  • Stay upright for 4 hours; no rubbing/massage that day
  • We include a 10–14 day review to fine-tune if appropriate

Contraindications & Cautions

Not suitable during pregnancy/breastfeeding, in certain neuromuscular conditions, or with active infection at injection sites. We’ll check your eligibility during consultation.
