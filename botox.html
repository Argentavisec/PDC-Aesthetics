What Does Botox Help With?

Botox can be used to reduce the appearance of common lines and wrinkles including:

  • Forehead lines
  • Frown lines (glabellar lines)
  • Crow’s feet (around the eyes)
  • Neck bands
  • Bunny lines (nose wrinkles)
  • Gummy smiles

We’ll assess your facial structure and discuss your goals before recommending a personalised plan. Every client receives a thorough consultation and post-treatment advice.

What to Expect During a Botox Appointment

Your appointment will include a full consultation and consent process. We'll clean the treatment areas and use ultra-fine needles to inject Botox into specific facial muscles. The process usually takes 15–30 minutes.

Botox begins to take effect within 3 to 5 days, with full results visible at 10 to 14 days. Effects typically last 3 to 4 months. You can return to your daily routine immediately — there’s minimal downtime.

Common Botox Questions

Is Botox painful?
Discomfort is very minimal. Most clients describe it as a small pinch. We use precise, gentle techniques to minimise sensation.

Will I look frozen?
Not at all. Our focus is softening lines while keeping expression and movement natural. You’ll still look like you – just refreshed.

Can Botox prevent wrinkles?
Yes – regular Botox can reduce the depth of existing wrinkles and help prevent new ones from forming.
