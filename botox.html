How Botox Works

Botox temporarily relaxes the targeted facial muscles that cause wrinkles. The procedure is quick, relatively painless, and requires no downtime. Results appear gradually within 3–7 days and typically last 3–4 months.

You’ll receive a consultation before your first treatment to assess your suitability and tailor your dosage and areas.

Who Is Botox Suitable For?

Botox is ideal for clients looking to:

  • Minimise the signs of ageing
  • Prevent future wrinkle formation
  • Improve facial symmetry
  • Achieve a fresher, well-rested appearance

It’s suitable for adults in good general health. All treatments are carried out following a full medical consultation and assessment.

FAQs – Botox at Home

Is Botox safe?

Yes. Botox is one of the most studied cosmetic procedures in the world. When administered by a medical professional, it’s extremely safe and effective.

How long does the treatment take?

Most appointments take 20–30 minutes including consultation and preparation.

Will it look natural?

Absolutely. We specialise in natural-looking, subtle results that simply enhance your features. No frozen faces here!

Does it hurt?

Most clients experience only mild discomfort — similar to a pinch. The needles used are very fine and the treatment is quick.

How soon will I see results?

Results begin to appear within 3–5 days and peak around 10–14 days.
