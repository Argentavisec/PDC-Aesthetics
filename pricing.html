Lip Filler Prices (0.5ml vs 1ml)

Popular searches include “lip filler price 0.5ml”, “1ml lip filler cost near me” and “natural lip filler Stone”. We recommend the volume that best fits your lip anatomy and goals:

  • 0.5ml: subtle hydration/shape; good for first-timers or refinement.
  • 1ml: more definition or correction; still natural-looking with our conservative approach.
  • Plans: staged top-ups to build gradually; full aftercare and 2-week review window.

Anti-Wrinkle (Botox) Prices per Area

Common queries: “Botox price one area”, “forehead Botox cost”, “crow’s feet price near me”. We tailor dose conservatively and price by area:

  • Typical areas: frown (glabella), forehead, crow’s feet; add-ons like bunny lines or lip flip if suitable.
  • Timeline: results begin 3–7 days; settle by ~2 weeks; usually last ~3–4 months.
  • Review: we can reassess at ~2 weeks and adjust if appropriate.
