Frequently Asked Questions – Mobile Service Coverage

What if I don’t live exactly in one of the towns listed?

No problem — we regularly travel to nearby areas as well. Just contact us and we’ll let you know if we can reach you.

Is there an extra fee for mobile visits?

No added cost for clients within our 15-mile radius. For longer distances, a small travel fee may apply, which we’ll confirm upfront.

How do I book a treatment in my area?

You can message us here or via our social media. We'll confirm availability, explain the process, and get you booked in at a time that suits you.

Do you offer treatments for groups?

Yes! We offer group appointments, which are great for friends or family who want to receive treatments in one visit. Group discounts may apply.

Is your mobile service safe?

Absolutely. Our nurse is fully qualified, and all tools and surfaces are sterilised before every visit. We bring a professional, discreet clinic setup to you.
